Whats Prodigo.?

Incessant Integration for Inspiring Education

A enterprise resource planning (E.R.P) platform with many modules that can handle various tasks of various departments of your institution. Few of the modules being

  • Attendance
  • Student Syllabus
  • Schedule
  • Overall improvement
  • Class schedule automation 
  • Assignments and much more..

Set to fill the communication gap between staff and students while taking the institute to new heights.
